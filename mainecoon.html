Maine Coon

The Maine Coon is one of the largest domesticated cat breeds. They are known for their friendly and playful nature. Maine Coons are also known for their tufted ears and bushy tails.

Characteristics

  • Origin: United States
  • Life Span: 10-13 years
  • Temperament: Friendly, playful, and intelligent
  • Coat: Long, thick, and water-resistant

Care Tips for Maine Coons

  • Brush their coat regularly to prevent matting.
  • Provide plenty of playtime and mental stimulation.
  • Ensure they have a balanced diet to support their large size.
  • Regular vet check-ups are essential.
  • Provide a large and sturdy scratching post.
